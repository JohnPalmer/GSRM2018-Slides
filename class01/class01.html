Course Blueprint

 

Schedule

  • Lectures (Tuesdays & Thursdays)

Evaluation

  • Quizzes (15%)
  • Attendance and participation in seminars (5%)
  • Research Project (35%)
  • Final Exam (45%)
 

Quizzes

  • Short
  • During lecture
  • On the reading assigned for that day
  • Some announced, some not
  • First one will be during next lecture (Thursday)
 

Seminars

  • Taught by Wanli Nie
  • Opportunity to ask questions and discuss material in smaller group
  • Hands-on practice with methods
  • Progress with research project
 

Research Project

  • Develop and present a research proposal
  • Short research assignments will be due before each seminar and discussed there
  • Short oral presentation of proposal in final seminar
  • Grade based on (1) oral presentation, (2) portfolio of research assignments, and (3) 4-page written proposal
 

Final Exam

  • Traditional written exam
  • Short-answer questions
  • Date and room to be announced
